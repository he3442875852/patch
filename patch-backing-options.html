Patch Backing Options

Backing affects how the patch is applied, removed, washed, and priced. Choose based on garment, use case, and durability needs.

Sew-On

Most durable for uniforms, jackets, bags, and long-term use.

Iron-On

Easy application for apparel, event patches, and light merchandise.

Velcro

Removable option for tactical, morale, uniform, and gear patches.

Adhesive

Temporary application for packaging, events, or display use.

Pin

Good for removable badges, event access, and promotional wear.

No Backing

Used when the patch will be sewn into production by another factory.
