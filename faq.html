Common questions answered

Custom Patches FAQ

Quick answers about artwork, materials, backing, borders, minimum quantities, proofs, pricing, and shipping.

Can you make patches from my logo?

Yes. Send your logo, sketch, or reference image and we will recommend the best production method.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQ depends on patch type, size, and complexity. Share your target quantity for a quote.

Can I approve a proof first?

Yes. A digital proof should be approved before production.

Which backing should I choose?

Sew-on is durable, iron-on is convenient, and velcro is best for removable patches.

Do you ship internationally?

Shipping can be quoted based on destination, quantity, package weight, and deadline.

Which patch type is best?

Embroidered is classic, PVC is durable, woven is detailed, chenille is soft, leather is premium, and printed is best for full color.
