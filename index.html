Not sure which patch type to choose?

Send your artwork and tell us how the patch will be used. We can recommend embroidered, PVC, woven, chenille, leather, or printed based on texture, detail, durability, and budget.

  • Embroidered: classic stitched look
  • PVC: waterproof and durable
  • Woven: small text and fine detail
  • Printed: full color artwork

Ask for Recommendation

Can I get a proof before production?

Yes. A digital proof should be reviewed before production so shape, colors, backing, and details are clear.

What information is needed for a quote?

Artwork, patch type, size, quantity, backing, border, deadline, and shipping country.

Which backing is best?

Sew-on is durable, iron-on is convenient, and velcro is best for removable uniform or tactical patches.

Can you help with only an idea?

Yes. Send a sketch or reference image and we can guide the material and layout.
