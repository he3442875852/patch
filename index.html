FAQ

Quick answers for common custom patch inquiries.

Can you make patches from my logo?

Yes. Send your logo, sketch, or reference image and we will review the best production method.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQ depends on patch type, size, and complexity. Share your target quantity and we will quote accordingly.

Can I approve a proof first?

Yes. A digital proof should be confirmed before production so the shape, colors, and details are clear.

Do you ship worldwide?

The site is prepared for international inquiries. Shipping details can be quoted based on destination and deadline.

Which backing should I choose?

Sew-on is durable, iron-on is easy to apply, and velcro works well for removable uniform or tactical patches.

Can you help if I am not sure which patch type I need?

Yes. Describe the use case and we can recommend the best material, backing, and finish.
